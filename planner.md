### 🗺️ **Your Detailed 11-Night Itinerary**

This itinerary is crafted to immerse you in Kenya's iconic wildlife and vibrant culture at a comfortable pace.

#### **Part 1: Nairobi - Culture & Conservation 🏙️**

**Day 1: Sunday, Oct 19 - Arrival & Acclimatization**
*   **Morning:** Arrive at NBO (12:45 AM). After clearing immigration, transfer to the **Tamarind Tree Hotel** for a well-deserved rest.
*   **Afternoon:** Enjoy a leisurely start. At 2:30 PM, visit the **Giraffe Centre** for an up-close and personal experience hand-feeding endangered Rothschild's giraffes.
*   **Evening:** Experience the iconic **Carnivore Restaurant** for a "Nyama Choma" (barbecued meat) feast.

**Day 2: Monday, Oct 20 - Urban Wildlife & Local Crafts**
*   **Morning:** Visit the **Sheldrick Wildlife Trust** (11 AM - 12 PM) to see the adorable baby elephants during their feeding time.
*   **Afternoon:** Step back in time with a guided tour of the **Karen Blixen Museum**, followed by souvenir hunting at the vibrant, open-air **Maasai Market**.
*   **Evening:** Enjoy dinner at the highly-rated **Talisman Restaurant** in Karen.

**Day 3: Tuesday, Oct 21 - Community & Safari Preparation**
*   **Morning:** Visit the **Kazuri Beads Factory**, a wonderful social enterprise supporting local women.
*   **Afternoon:** Immerse yourself in Kenya's heritage with traditional music and dance performances at the **Bomas of Kenya**.
*   **Evening:** Relax at the hotel and pack for your safari adventure.

#### **Part 2: Maasai Mara - The Ultimate Safari Experience 🦁**

**Day 4: Wednesday, Oct 22 - Journey to the Savannah**
*   **Morning:** After an early breakfast, begin the scenic 6-hour road journey to the Maasai Mara, stopping at the **Great Rift Valley Viewpoint**.
*   **Afternoon:** Arrive at **Basecamp Masai Mara**. After lunch, embark on your first **evening game drive** in the conservancy.
*   **Evening:** Enjoy a classic safari **sundowner** in the bush as you watch the sunset, followed by dinner at the camp.

**Day 5: Thursday, Oct 23 - Full Day of Game Viewing**
*   **All Day:** Spend the day exploring the Maasai Mara with **morning and evening game drives**. The cooler hours are the best time to spot predators. In between, relax at the camp, which overlooks the Talek River.

**Day 6: Friday, Oct 24 - Deep Mara Immersion & Maasai Culture**
*   **Option 1:** Embark on a **full-day game drive** into the main National Reserve with a packed picnic lunch to explore further afield.
*   **Option 2:** After a morning game drive, participate in an authentic and respectful **Maasai community visit** arranged by Basecamp to learn about their traditional way of life.

**Day 7: Saturday, Oct 25 - A Different Perspective**
*   **Morning (Optional):** Take a once-in-a-lifetime **Hot Air Balloon Safari** at sunrise, followed by a champagne bush breakfast. (Note: Additional cost). Alternatively, enjoy a final morning game drive or a guided nature walk.
*   **Afternoon:** Relax and soak in the final sights and sounds of the African bush.

#### **Part 3: Lake Naivasha & Departure 🦛**

**Day 8: Sunday, Oct 26 - From Savannah to Serene Lake**
*   **Morning:** After a final breakfast, drive approximately 4-5 hours to Lake Naivasha.
*   **Afternoon:** Check into **Lake Naivasha Sopa Resort**. Spend the rest of the day exploring the beautiful grounds, where giraffes, waterbucks, and monkeys roam freely.

**Day 9: Monday, Oct 27 - Active Lake Adventures**
*   **Morning:** Take a **boat safari on Lake Naivasha** to see hippos and diverse birdlife, followed by a **walking safari** among giraffes and zebras on **Crescent Island**.
*   **Afternoon:** Explore **Hell's Gate National Park**, famous for its stunning gorge landscapes. You can hire bicycles and ride through the park.

**Day 10: Tuesday, Oct 28 - Return to Nairobi & Farewell**
*   **Morning:** Enjoy a leisurely breakfast and check out. Begin the 2.5-hour drive back to Nairobi.
*   **Afternoon:** Do some last-minute souvenir shopping at the **Utamaduni Craft Centre**.
*   **Evening:** Enjoy a wonderful **farewell dinner** before your late transfer to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (NBO).

**Day 11: Wednesday, Oct 29 - Departure**
*   **Early Morning:** Your flight (TK 608) departs at 2:30 AM, filled with incredible memories.

### 🌍 **Essential Kenya Travel Guide**

**Currency & Tipping 💵**
*   **Currency:** Kenyan Shilling (KES). Approx. **$1 USD = 130 KES**.
*   **Cards & Cash:** Credit cards are widely accepted in hotels and restaurants. Carry KES cash for markets, snacks, and tips.
*   **Tipping:** Tipping is customary. Budget **$10-15 USD per person per day** for your safari guide and **$5-10 USD per person per day** for the camp's communal staff tip box. Tip 10% at restaurants.

**Weather in Late October 🌦️**
*   You'll be visiting during the **"short rains"**. This means lush, green landscapes and fewer crowds. Expect warm, pleasant days (**70-85°F / 21-29°C**) with potential for short, heavy afternoon or evening showers.
*   **Layering is key!** Mornings and evenings, especially on safari, can be cool. Pack a fleece or warm jacket.

**Safety & Health ❤️**
*   **Health:** Consult a travel doctor 4-6 weeks before your trip for recommended vaccinations and malaria prophylaxis.
*   **Water:** **Do not drink tap water.** Stick to bottled water provided by your accommodations.
*   **Safari Safety:** Always listen to your guide. **Never walk alone at night** in the unfenced safari camp; always request an escort.
*   **Insurance:** Comprehensive travel insurance with medical evacuation coverage is mandatory.

**Cultural Etiquette 🙏**
*   **Greetings:** Always start interactions with a friendly greeting. **"Jambo!"** (Hello!) is common, or try **"Habari?"** (How are you?).
*   **Photography:** **ALWAYS ask for permission** before taking a photo of a person. It is considered very rude not to.
*   **Dress Code:** Casual clothing is fine for safari. In Nairobi and when visiting communities, dress modestly (covering shoulders and knees) out of respect.
