### 🌍 Local Guide: Essential Nairobi Know-How

#### **💰 Currency & Money**
*   The local currency is the **Kenyan Shilling (KES)**. The approximate rate is **$1 USD ≈ 130 KES** (check current rates before travel).
*   Exchange a small amount of cash at the airport and the rest at a Forex Bureau in a mall for better rates.
*   **Credit cards (Visa/Mastercard)** are widely accepted at hotels and restaurants. Inform your bank of your travel dates.

#### **🌦️ Weather & Packing (Late October)**
*   Expect Kenya's "short rains" season: lush green landscapes with warm, pleasant days **(66°F-79°F)** and a chance of short, heavy afternoon showers.
*   **Pack layers:** Lightweight shirts, a fleece for cool mornings/evenings, and a light rain jacket.
*   **For safaris, wear neutral colors** (khaki, beige, green). Avoid bright colors.
*   **Essentials:** Comfortable walking shoes, a wide-brimmed hat, sunglasses, and high-SPF sunscreen.

#### **🛡️ Health & Safety**
*   **Transportation:** While you mentioned public transport, for safety and efficiency, we strongly recommend and have budgeted for **Uber and Bolt**. These ride-sharing apps are reliable, safe, and very affordable in Nairobi. Avoid walking alone at night.
*   **Water:** Drink only bottled or filtered water.
*   **Vaccinations:** Consult your doctor well in advance about recommended vaccinations and medications.

#### **🤝 Cultural Etiquette**
*   **Greetings are key:** Always start interactions with *"Jambo"* (Hello) or *"Habari?"* (How are you?).
*   **Ask for permission** before taking photos of people. Many will expect a small tip.
*   **Tipping:** 10% is appreciated at restaurants. For safari guides, $10-$15 USD per person per day is a good guideline.
*   **Bargaining** is expected at open-air markets but not in formal shops.

#### **🗣️ Basic Swahili Phrases**
Learning a few words will go a long way!
*   **Jambo!** - Hello!
*   **Habari?** - How are you? (Reply: **Mzuri** - Good)
*   **Asante** - Thank you ( **Asante sana** - Thank you very much)
*   **Karibu** - Welcome
*   **Sawa** - Okay / Alright
*   **Hakuna Matata** - No worries!
